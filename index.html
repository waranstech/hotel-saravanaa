Our Story

A Taste of Tradition

🍛

Welcome to Hotel Saravanaa, a beloved culinary destination in Nanganallur, Chennai. We proudly serve authentic South Indian cuisine that captures the true essence of Tamil Nadu's rich food heritage.

With two dedicated branches — one exclusively vegetarian and one non-vegetarian — we cater to every palate with the same commitment to quality, freshness, and authentic flavours that our customers have loved for years.

From our signature crispy dosas and fluffy idlis to aromatic biryanis and spicy chicken specialties, every dish is prepared with carefully selected ingredients and traditional recipes passed down through generations.
